# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** August 23, 2025

FrostByte Studios (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our services, including our Tebex store, GitHub projects, and Discord community.

### 1. Information We Collect

We only collect limited information necessary to provide and improve our services:

* **Tebex Purchases**: When you buy a script, Tebex processes your payment information. We only see purchase details (what was bought, when, and your email for delivery/support).
* **Discord**: If you join our Discord, we see your Discord username and messages you choose to share.
* **GitHub**: If you interact with our repositories, GitHub may share basic profile information (public username, contributions).

We do **not** collect sensitive personal data (like passwords, addresses, or payment card details).

### 2. How We Use Information

We use this information to:

* Deliver your purchased scripts and updates.
* Provide support and answer questions.
* Prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse of our resources.
* Improve our services and community experience.

### 3. Data Sharing

* We do not sell or rent your data.
* Payment details are handled securely by **Tebex** and their payment providers.
* We may share data if required by law or to enforce our Terms of Service.

### 4. Your Rights

You can request to:

* Access or delete your purchase history.
* Leave our Discord at any time.
* Remove your GitHub contributions by editing/deleting them directly on GitHub.
